Our Mission

Our mission is to embody Big Dog Energy: strength to protect pups who need a home, bravery to stand up for those who can't stand up for themselves, and love for fun and spreading joy.

About Us

B.D.E. was founded in 2018 - just four friends and one big dream of caring for dogs in need and enriching the lives of homes that need a new friend. Now, two years later, we've rescued over 1,000 dogs! Each day is hard work - unfortunately, there are so many dogs that need help in our city. But with the help of our dedicated team and the support of our community, we've been happily able to help out so many pups in need, and so many more to come! Dogs are love, dogs are life, dogs are everything.
