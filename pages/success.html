Success Stories

Our crew has matched hundreds of dogs with new owners. These are a few of their stories.

Meet Frida

Frida is a top-down kind of gal. She loves to go on rides in an old-school VW Bug with her new owner. Her life wasn't always the one of glamour though; she was rescued from under a freeway interchange with her fur matted to the point of unrecognizability and badly in need of a clean. We gave her the haircut and bath she deserved, and within hours, she transformed into the beauty you see here.

Greet Remy

Remy can't resist a good leaf. His enjoyment of life is what made his new owners fall for him. Rescued from a puppy breeder with little regard for dogs' lives, Remy was hand-nursed by one of our dedicated staff until he was ready to be adopted. When his new family walked into our shelter, they couldn't resist his stubby little legs and adorable fluffy tail. We were sad to see him go, but now Remy goes on hikes and has picnics in the park, so it's a win-win for everyone involved.

Say hello to Luna

Luna is the epitome of grace. She loves running through snow at full speed and has the winningest smile while doing it. Luna was turned in to us by a family who was moving overseas and couldn't take her with them. She wasn't in our shelter for long (I mean, look at that face!), and is now happily residing in the countryside with lots of room to run around.

Look out for Pickles

Pickles may be a small dog, but he is the biggest goofball. His favorite pastimes are rolling in grass and sticking his head out the window of moving cars. Pickles almost didn't make it to us, though. His previous owner was going to put him down when there wasn't enough money to pay for the veterinary bill for his arthritis, but luckily we learned about him in time to bring him to the shelter. We got him all fixed up and now he's good as new, ready to take on the rest of what life throws at him.
